Meeting the needs of patients

Providing a remote consultation platform where patients can interact with health care professionals through an intelligent chatbot interface promoting safe and accessibole healthcare service

Register Now

The Patient-Doctor Chatbot project aims to address the challenge of overcrowding and long wait times in hospitals in Ghana's healthcare systems.

Many individuals are discouraged from visiting hospitals to seek medical care due to the inconvenience and frustration of long wait times, leading them to resort to self-medication practices which poses significant risks.
